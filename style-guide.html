Brand Information

What does the company do, or offer?

Solaris is a (fictional) brand that produces classic and high quality space-exploration footwear.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Solaris is a trusted provider of fashionable and long lasting pieces suitable for work, leisure, and formal occasions.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

Customer identity

  • Space travellers/workers
  • Looking to spend money on the best quality pieces, created to last in terms of durability and style.
  • Want something more fashionable beyond standard issued equipment. Something for special events/spice up the everyday wardrobe

Initial Inspiration

  • Retrofuturism
  • In a setting where people could travel through space or visit planets, what would they wear?
  • Unisex, minimalist fashion
  • NASA, Visions of the Future poster series:
